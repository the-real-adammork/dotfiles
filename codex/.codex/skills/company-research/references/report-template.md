# Company Research Report Template

Use this template for the final brief saved at `docs/breifs/<companyname>/brief.md`. Keep it concise unless the user asks for a deep report.

- Company:
- URL:
- One-line description:
- Category:
- Headquarters / operating geography:
- Business model:

- Market size estimate:

## What The Company Does

Explain the company's core problem, buyer/user, workflow, and value proposition in plain language. Distinguish official positioning from your interpretation.

## Products

List each product, module, platform, API, service line, marketplace offering, or plan discovered.

For each product include:

- What it does
- Target user or buyer
- Pricing or packaging signal if available
- Evidence source
- Confidence level if inferred

## Market

Summarize:

- Market category and definition
- TAM/SAM/SOM or best available market-size proxy
- Growth drivers
- Competitors and substitutes
- Constraints or risks
- Confidence and methodology

## Employee Count And Hiring Needs

Summarize:

- Employee count estimate and source basis
- Open roles by function
- Repeated skills or seniority patterns
- Hiring locations / remote policy
- What hiring implies about company priorities

## Technology Stack

Separate confirmed from likely stack.

Include:

- Frontend / mobile
- Backend / infrastructure
- Data / analytics / AI
- Security / compliance
- Integrations, APIs, SDKs, and developer ecosystem
- Evidence basis for each claim

## Ideal Customer Profile

Describe:

- Firmographics
- Buyer personas
- End users
- Pain points
- Buying triggers
- Required integrations or compliance needs
- Expansion potential

## Future Market Opportunities

Rank the top opportunities by evidence and strategic fit. For each include:

- Opportunity
- Why it fits
- Evidence
- Risks
- Confidence

## Open Questions

List unresolved questions that would require interviews, paid databases, private data, or direct company confirmation.

## Sources

Group sources by:

- Official company sources
- Primary ecosystem sources
- Secondary sources
- Inference-only evidence
